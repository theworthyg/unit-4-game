Infinity Stone Collector
How to Play the Infinity Gauntlet Game:
- Thanos will choose a random number at the start of the game.
- There are four Infinity Stones below. By clicking on a Stone, you will add an amount of points to your total score.
- You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
- The value of each Stone will be hidden until you click on it.
- Every time the game starts, the values of each Stone will change.

Click on the Infinity Stones to Play!
